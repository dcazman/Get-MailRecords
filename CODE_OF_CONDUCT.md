# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub Issues, Discussions, Pull Requests, and any other community interaction related to this project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/dcazman/Get-MailRecords/discussions) or contact the maintainer directly via GitHub.

All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion.

## Enforcement

The project maintainer reserves the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and to temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behavior deemed harmful.
